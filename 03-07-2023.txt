Even or Odd
----------------
n=int(input("Enter a Number:"))
if n%2==0:
  print("Even Number")
else:
  print("Odd Number")



Greatest Number
----------------------

a=int(input("Enter a First Number:"))
b=int(input("Enter a Second Number:"))
c=int(input("Enter a Third Number:"))
if a>b and a>c:
  print("a is greater")
elif b>c:
  print("b is greater")
else:
  print("c is greater")
